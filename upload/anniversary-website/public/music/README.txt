🎵 MUSIC FILES — HOW TO ADD YOUR SONGS

Place your .mp3 music files in this folder with these exact names:

  home.mp3       → Plays on the Homepage
  chapter1.mp3   → Plays on Chapter I (How It All Began)
  chapter2.mp3   → Plays on Chapter II (Growing Together)
  chapter3.mp3   → Plays on Chapter III (Our Favourite Moments)
  chapter4.mp3   → Plays on Chapter IV (Looking Forward)
  letter.mp3     → Plays on the Love Letter page

Tips:
- You can find free romantic music on sites like:
    https://pixabay.com/music/  (royalty-free)
    https://freemusicarchive.org/
- Just rename your chosen songs to match the filenames above
- MP3 format works best
- Keep files under 10MB for fast loading

Each page will automatically try to play its assigned song.
The player appears in the bottom-right corner — she can pause/play/adjust volume!
